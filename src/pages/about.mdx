## About this blog

Hi, I’m Kevin, Outdoor enthusiast and peak hiker.

This is my blog dedicated to my adventures in the outdoor.  Here I’ll be listing and cataloging peak hikes in the Pacific Northwest and elsewhere.  In the Blog section, I'll discuss different aspects of Hiking and recommend some things to help you be a peak hiker.
