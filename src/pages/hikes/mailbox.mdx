## Mailbox Peak

<Map title="Mailbox" mapstyle="mapbox://styles/kevek/ckyhun2330gf214pdia09g6qj" Lat={47.4642} Lng={-121.6585} bearing={70} pitch={50} zoom={13} />

Mailbox peak is named so because of the mailbox that sits at the top of the peak.  There are 2 trails up the mountain, one is newer and has many switchbacks making it longer, and the other is the old mailbox peak trail which is considered one of the hardest in the area dues to its steepness.
