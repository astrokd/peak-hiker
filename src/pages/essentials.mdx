## The Ten Essentials

The Ten Essentials are 10 categories you need to be prepared for when you head out anywhere but especially if you are heading into the wilderness.

My list below is based of the National Park Service list found here: <a href="https://www.nps.gov/articles/10essentials.htm" target="_blank">NPS.gov: Article: Ten Essentials</a>
